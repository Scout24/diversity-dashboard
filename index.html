  • 2017

  • March, 2017

    Diversity Dashboard

    To track our goal and make our current situation transparent we created this dashboard for everyone inside the company.

  • 2016

  • September, 2016

    Anti-Bias Workshop

    The Diversity in Tech Community members attended an anti bias workshop we hosted in our office.
  • June, 2016

    Community Offsite

    We took one day to talk about us as a community, to talk about the latest results of the second survey and most importantly, to set a goal that we could measure and work towards in the next year. The Goal: "Double the percentage of female engineers by the end of 2017"
  • June, 2016

    Second Survey

    We took the same survey from last year, applied some minor changes, and run it again to see how the opinions of our colleagues might have changed.
  • May, 2016

    Sticks & Stones

    We were present with a booth at the Career Fair „Sticks and Stones“ which focusses on the LGBT Community.
  • April, 2016

    Girls' Day

    During the Girls' Day we opened our doors both at IS24 and AS24 to those who wanted to get insights of what it means to be a software developer. Together with many volunteering Scouties we introduced the basics of website programming with HTML and CSS to 24 attending girls.
  • January, 2016

    Workshop for Hiring Managers

    We had a workshop for all interested Hiring Managers about bias in the hiring process and what to do about it.

  • 2015

  • November, 2015

    Diversity Claim

    In order to address more people from underrepresented groups we created a diversty claim in cooperation with HR.
  • June, 2015

    Our first Survey

    In order to figure out which challenges IS24 was facing, we sent out a survey to the builders community. Based on the outcome we were able to define what we should focus on first.
  • February, 2015

    Start!

    Once upon a time some Scouties brainstormed about the lack of diversity in tech in general and realized that this was also a challenge IS24 was facing at that moment. – This is how our Diversity in Tech Community was created.
